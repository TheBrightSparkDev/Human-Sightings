Who am I?

‘Human Sightings’ is a small, independent, art platform set up by myself in 2017. Throughout my life I’ve used art to cope with my own mental health struggles, as well as a way to explore gender identity.

I decided to start sharing my artwork a few years ago, and it’s been really amazing to see how many people can identify with the cynical aliens and slightly morbid but encouraging astronauts!

It makes my day every time I get a lovely message from someone about a badge they wear, or a postcard they’ve stuck on their wall!

It’s definitely made me feel less alone

- Evie Basch
